# DOFH

## Digital Officer From Hell

The Digital Officer From Hell appears wherever technology, bureaucracy and common sense collide.

Nobody knows which department employs him.

Nobody knows who he reports to.

Nobody knows how he remains employed.

Yet every citizen has encountered him.

The stories in this repository are works of satire.

Any resemblance to actual ministries, departments, agencies, corporations, portals, examination authorities, cybersecurity organizations, internet service providers, vendors, consultants, committees or digital transformation initiatives is entirely coincidental.
