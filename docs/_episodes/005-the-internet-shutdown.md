# DOFH #005
## The Internet Shutdown

The Junior Officer ran into the office.

"Sir, attackers are using the internet."

The Officer nodded gravely.

"This is serious."

"Very serious."

The Officer picked up the emergency phone.

Five minutes later the internet disappeared.

The Junior Officer looked relieved.

"Problem solved?"

"No."

"The attackers still have internet."

"Yes."

"The banks don't."

"Correct."

"The schools don't."

"Correct."

"The hospitals don't."

"Correct."

"The citizens don't."

"Correct."

The Junior Officer hesitated.

"Then what exactly have we achieved?"

The Digital Officer From Hell smiled.

"Control."

"Control of what?"

The Officer thought for a moment.

"That remains under assessment."

The Junior Officer looked out of the window.

The city had gone silent.

"Will the internet return soon?"

"Certainly."

"When?"

The Officer checked the draft press release.

"'As soon as normalcy is restored.'"

"What does that mean?"

The Officer smiled.

"It means nobody knows."
