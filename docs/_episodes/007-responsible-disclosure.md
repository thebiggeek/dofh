# DOFH #007
## Responsible Disclosure

The Junior Officer entered carrying a printed email.

"Sir, the researcher has reported another vulnerability."

The Digital Officer From Hell nodded.

"Has he disclosed it responsibly?"

"Yes."

"Excellent."

"He sent detailed documentation."

"Excellent."

"He provided proof of concept."

"Excellent."

"He explained how to fix it."

"Excellent."

The Junior Officer looked hopeful.

"So we'll fix it?"

The Officer looked confused.

"Why would we do that?"

"Because he reported it."

"Young man."

"Yes sir."

"The purpose of Responsible Disclosure is to ensure responsibility is properly disclosed."

"To whom?"

The Officer pointed around the room.

"Not to us."

A week passed.

Then two weeks.

Then three.

The researcher sent another email.

Then another.

Then another.

The Junior Officer looked concerned.

"Sir, should we reply?"

"Certainly."

The Officer opened a template.

"Thank you for your valuable contribution to cybersecurity."

"That's it?"

"That's it."

"But we haven't done anything."

The Officer smiled.

"Exactly."
