# DOFH #008
## The Advisory

"Sir, the vulnerability has become public."

The Digital Officer From Hell sighed.

"Release an advisory."

The Junior Officer nodded.

"What should it say?"

The Officer began dictating.

'Organizations are advised to remain vigilant.'

"About what?"

"Everything."

'Users should follow cybersecurity best practices.'

"Which practices?"

"The best ones."

The Junior Officer continued typing.

'All stakeholders should take appropriate precautions.'

"What precautions?"

The Officer looked offended.

"Young man."

"Yes sir."

"An advisory that contains specific instructions creates accountability."

The Junior Officer deleted the question.

The advisory was released.

Thousands of organizations received it.

Nobody knew what to do.

The Officer smiled.

"Perfect."
