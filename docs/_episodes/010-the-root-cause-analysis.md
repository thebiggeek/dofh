# DOFH #010
## The Root Cause Analysis

The incident had finally concluded.

The website was back online.

The dashboard was green.

The advisory had been issued.

The committee had met.

The Digital Officer From Hell entered the conference room.

"Time for the Root Cause Analysis."

The Junior Officer nodded.

"What was the root cause?"

The Officer opened the report.

The first page read:

Unexpected circumstances.

"That's not a root cause."

The Officer turned the page.

Contributing factors.

"What factors?"

Complex technical conditions.

The Junior Officer sighed.

The Officer continued.

Lessons learned.

"What lessons?"

Improved coordination is required.

The Junior Officer buried his face in his hands.

"Sir, are we ever going to explain what actually happened?"

The Officer looked horrified.

"Certainly not."

"Why?"

"Because then we might learn something."

The room fell silent.

The Officer signed the report.

The incident was officially closed.

Nothing had changed.

Everything was documented.

The process had succeeded.
