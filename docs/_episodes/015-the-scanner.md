# DOFH #015

## The Scanner

The transformation programme had entered its next phase.

Digitization.

Millions of records.

Millions of pages.

Millions of opportunities.

The Junior Officer entered carrying a sample document.

"Sir, we have a problem."

The Digital Officer From Hell looked up.

"What sort of problem?"

"The scanner cannot read this page."

The Officer examined it.

"Can the page be scanned?"

"Yes."

"Excellent."

The Junior Officer hesitated.

"But it cannot be read."

The Officer looked confused.

"The requirement is scanning."

"Not readability?"

"No."

The Junior Officer tried again.

"The image is blurry."

"Scanned."

"The text is missing."

"Scanned."

"The page is upside down."

"Scanned."

"The image is black."

The Officer nodded.

"Still scanned."

Weeks later the progress report arrived.

DOCUMENTS DIGITIZED

100%

The Officer smiled.

"Excellent."

The Junior Officer looked worried.

"Nobody can actually read half of them."

The Officer shrugged.

"That is an access problem."

"I thought this was an access project."

"It is."

The Officer pointed at the report.

"Before digitization, people could not access the records."

"Yes."

"After digitization, people still cannot access the records."

"Yes."

The Officer spread his hands.

"The outcome remains stable."

The Junior Officer buried his face in his hands.
