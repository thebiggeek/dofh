# DOFH #016

## The Wrong Record

The first complaint arrived on a Tuesday.

The second arrived minutes later.

By lunchtime there were hundreds.

The Junior Officer rushed into the office.

"Sir."

The Digital Officer From Hell looked up.

"Yes?"

"Users are receiving the wrong records."

The Officer nodded.

"How wrong?"

"They belong to other people."

The Officer considered this.

"Interesting."

"Interesting?"

"Data is clearly moving through the system."

The Junior Officer stared.

"Sir, people are seeing records that are not theirs."

The Officer leaned back.

"That demonstrates interoperability."

The Junior Officer blinked.

"I don't think it does."

The Officer ignored him.

By afternoon the issue had become widespread.

The support desk was overwhelmed.

The technical team was alarmed.

The vendor was investigating.

The Officer remained calm.

"How many users are affected?"

"We don't know."

"Excellent."

The Junior Officer sighed.

"What do we tell them?"

The Officer thought carefully.

"Tell them the platform is currently providing an enhanced cross-reference experience."

The Junior Officer stared silently.

"You just made that up."

The Officer smiled.

"That is how innovation begins."
