# DOFH #017

## The Portal

The Portal had become the public face of the transformation programme.

Unfortunately, it had also become its public face.

The Junior Officer entered carrying monitoring reports.

"Sir."

The Digital Officer From Hell looked up.

"The Portal?"

"The Portal."

The Officer sighed.

"What now?"

"It is unavailable."

Again."

"Again."

The Officer examined the dashboard.

Every indicator was green.

"Looks fine."

The Junior Officer looked at his own screen.

The Portal was down.

The login service was down.

The support system was down.

The monitoring dashboard was green.

"Sir, the dashboard appears disconnected from reality."

The Officer smiled.

"Excellent."

"Excellent?"

"Reality changes constantly."

The Junior Officer stared.

"The dashboard provides stability."

By evening the Portal returned.

At midnight it failed again.

The next morning it recovered.

The dashboard never changed.

The Officer admired the consistency.

The Junior Officer admired nothing.

"Shouldn't the dashboard reflect what's happening?"

The Officer looked shocked.

"And alarm people unnecessarily?"
