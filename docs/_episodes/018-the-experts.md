# DOFH #018

## The Experts

Three months after deployment, the Experts arrived.

The Junior Officer greeted them at reception.

"Welcome."

The Experts nodded gravely.

Each carried a laptop.

Each carried a report.

Each carried the expression of someone who already knew the ending.

The Digital Officer From Hell entered the conference room.

"Excellent. The Experts are here."

The Junior Officer hesitated.

"Sir."

"Yes?"

"Why weren't they consulted before deployment?"

The Officer looked puzzled.

"Then they would merely have been participants."

The Junior Officer blinked.

"What are they now?"

The Officer smiled.

"Experts."

The Experts spent weeks reviewing the programme.

Eventually they produced a report.

The report identified:

* Capacity issues
* Process issues
* Security issues
* Governance issues
* Communication issues

The Junior Officer looked surprised.

"These are exactly the issues the technical team identified before launch."

The Officer nodded.

"Yes."

"Then why didn't we listen?"

The Officer looked offended.

"Young man."

"Yes sir."

"Those were merely concerns."

He held up the Expert Report.

"These are findings."

The Junior Officer made a note.

The distinction appeared expensive.
