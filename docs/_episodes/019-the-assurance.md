# DOFH #019

## The Assurance

Public concern continued to grow.

Questions were being asked.

Statements were being requested.

The Junior Officer entered carrying a draft.

"Sir, we need to reassure people."

The Digital Officer From Hell nodded.

"Prepare an assurance."

The Junior Officer looked at the situation report.

The Portal remained unstable.

Support queues remained overloaded.

The vendor remained concerned.

The Experts remained employed.

"What exactly should we assure them?"

The Officer thought carefully.

"Everything."

The Junior Officer began typing.

"There is no cause for concern."

The Officer nodded.

"Good."

"The situation is under control."

"Excellent."

"The issues have been addressed."

The Junior Officer paused.

"Have they?"

The Officer ignored the question.

The statement was released.

The media reported it.

The public discussed it.

The systems continued behaving exactly as before.

The Junior Officer looked confused.

"Sir, the assurance changed nothing."

The Officer smiled.

"An assurance is not intended to change reality."

"What is it intended to change?"

The Officer pointed at the headline.

"Tomorrow's news cycle."
