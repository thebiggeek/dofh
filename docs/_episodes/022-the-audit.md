# DOFH #022

## The Audit

Three months after the transformation programme concluded, the Audit began.

The auditors requested documents.

The Programme Office provided documents.

The auditors requested additional documents.

The Programme Office provided additional documents.

The auditors requested all emails relating to project risks.

The Programme Office suddenly became very busy.

The Junior Officer entered carrying a report.

"Sir."

The Digital Officer From Hell looked up.

"The Audit?"

"The Audit."

The Officer sighed.

"What have they found?"

The Junior Officer opened the report.

"The auditors found a risk assessment."

"Excellent."

"They found a capacity study."

"Excellent."

"They found a readiness review."

"Excellent."

"They found a pilot report."

"Excellent."

The Junior Officer continued reading.

"All of them identified the same issues."

The Officer nodded.

"Consistency is important."

The Junior Officer looked unconvinced.

"The same issues later occurred during deployment."

"Excellent."

"Excellent?"

The Officer smiled.

"The predictions were accurate."

The auditors continued their investigation.

Every warning had been documented.

Every concern had been recorded.

Every recommendation had been acknowledged.

The Audit Report eventually reached a startling conclusion.

The problems were not unexpected.

The Junior Officer stared.

"They weren't?"

"No."

"They were predicted?"

"Repeatedly."

"Then why wasn't anything done?"

The Officer looked offended.

"Young man."

"Yes sir."

"That would have changed the schedule."

The auditors produced their final recommendation.

Future warnings should be reviewed more carefully.

The recommendation was accepted unanimously.

A committee was immediately formed.

To review the recommendation.
