# DOFH #028

## The Migration

The Corrective Action Plan had been implemented successfully.

The dashboard was green.

The committees were active.

The reports were complete.

The problems remained.

The Junior Officer entered carrying a proposal.

"Sir."

The Digital Officer From Hell looked up.

"What now?"

"The Programme Office would like to migrate."

The Officer smiled.

"Excellent."

"To a new platform."

"Excellent."

The Junior Officer hesitated.

"Doesn't that mean the old platform failed?"

The room became silent.

The Vendor looked uncomfortable.

The Consultants became fascinated by their laptops.

The Programme Office examined the ceiling.

The Officer looked offended.

"Young man."

"Yes sir."

"We do not use the word failed."

The Junior Officer nodded.

"What word do we use?"

The Officer opened the proposal.

PHASE TWO MODERNIZATION

The Junior Officer read it.

"The entire system is being replaced."

"Enhanced."

"The architecture is changing."

"Modernized."

"The platform is being retired."

"Transitioned."

The Junior Officer looked confused.

"Sir."

"Yes?"

"If the platform was successful, why are we replacing it?"

The Officer smiled.

"If it had failed, we would also replace it."

The Junior Officer paused.

"How can I tell the difference?"

The Officer took a sip of tea.

"In one case we call it a migration."

The migration proceeded rapidly.

New vendors arrived.

New consultants arrived.

New dashboards arrived.

The old platform quietly disappeared.

The final project report was released.

The first line read:

THE PROGRAMME HAS BEEN SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED.

The second line announced:

A NEW TRANSFORMATION PROGRAMME WILL COMMENCE IMMEDIATELY.

The Junior Officer stared.

The Officer smiled.

Progress had been achieved.

The destination remained unchanged.
