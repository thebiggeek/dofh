# About DOFH

Digital Officer From Hell (DOFH) is a work of satire by **Gagandeep Singh Sapra**, commonly known as **the BigGeek**.

The stories explore the intersection of:

- Technology
- Bureaucracy
- Procurement
- Governance
- Committees
- Vendors
- Consultants
- Digital Transformation

All characters, organizations, incidents, systems, portals, tenders, advisories, dashboards, outages, committees, vulnerabilities, transformations and announcements depicted in these stories are fictional.

Any resemblance to actual persons, organizations, agencies, corporations, vendors, projects, portals, tenders, examination systems, cybersecurity incidents or public events is entirely coincidental.

## Inspiration

DOFH descends from the long tradition of technology and workplace satire — most directly the [Bastard Operator From Hell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bastard_Operator_From_Hell).
