# DOFH #020

## The Lessons Learned

Six months later the transformation programme concluded its review.

The conference room was full.

The Vendor attended.

The Experts attended.

The Consultants attended.

The Committees attended.

The Junior Officer attended.

The Digital Officer From Hell chaired the meeting.

The final report was presented.

Hundreds of pages.

Dozens of charts.

Countless recommendations.

The Junior Officer raised his hand.

"What lessons have we learned?"

The room fell silent.

The Vendor looked at the Consultants.

The Consultants looked at the Experts.

The Experts looked at the Committee.

The Committee looked at the Officer.

The Officer opened the report.

The final page contained the official conclusion.

The transformation programme had been successfully completed.

The Junior Officer stared.

"Successfully?"

"Yes."

"The Portal failed repeatedly."

"Yes."

"The records were incorrect."

"Yes."

"The experts identified serious issues."

"Yes."

"The public lost confidence."

"Yes."

The Junior Officer pointed at the report.

"Then how was it successful?"

The Officer smiled.

"The programme was delivered."

The Junior Officer looked confused.

"Delivered?"

"On schedule."

"And?"

"Within budget."

"And?"

The Officer closed the report.

"Most importantly, the programme has now entered the next phase."

The Junior Officer frowned.

"What next phase?"

The Officer slid a new folder across the table.

DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION 2.0

The Junior Officer slowly opened it.

The first page read:

LESSONS LEARNED FROM THE PREVIOUS TRANSFORMATION

The Officer smiled.

The cycle was ready to begin again.
