# DOFH #002
## The OTP

"Sir, citizens are complaining they are not receiving OTPs."

The Digital Officer From Hell nodded.

"A security success."

"Sir?"

"If nobody receives the OTP, nobody can log in."

"Yes."

"And if nobody can log in, nobody can compromise accounts."

The Junior Officer blinked.

"I don't think that's how security works."

The Officer leaned back.

"Can an attacker access an account without an OTP?"

"No."

"Can the citizen?"

"No."

"Then we have achieved parity."

The Junior Officer stared silently.

"Should we investigate the SMS gateway?"

"Certainly."

The Officer created a committee.

"What will they investigate?"

"Whether OTP stands for One Time Password."

"And after that?"

"A second committee."

"Why?"

"To determine why the first committee was necessary."

The Junior Officer wrote this down.

He was learning.
