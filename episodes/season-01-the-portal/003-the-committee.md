# DOFH #003
## The Committee

"Sir, the examination system has failed."

The Digital Officer From Hell nodded.

"Form a committee."

"Done."

"The committee submitted its findings."

"Form another committee."

"Done."

"The second committee agrees with the first."

The Officer frowned.

"That is concerning."

"Why?"

"If committees begin agreeing with each other, decisions may follow."

The Junior Officer looked alarmed.

"What should we do?"

"Create a High-Level Committee."

"What makes it high-level?"

"Book the conference room on the top floor."

The Junior Officer made a note.

The Officer continued.

"Remember this."

"Yes sir."

"Problems are temporary."

"Yes sir."

"Committees are forever."
