# DOFH #004
## The Leak

The Junior Officer entered carrying several printouts.

"Sir, the examination paper has leaked."

The Officer looked concerned.

"To whom?"

"Everyone."

The concern disappeared.

"Excellent."

"Sir?"

"If everybody has the paper, nobody has an unfair advantage."

The Junior Officer stared.

"That doesn't sound right."

"Neither does the examination system, yet here we are."

Social media was already exploding.

Students were angry.

Parents were angry.

News channels were angry.

The Officer remained calm.

"Prepare a statement."

"What should it say?"

The Officer recited from memory.

'Reports have been noted. The matter is being examined.'

"But sir, we already know what happened."

"Young man."

"Yes sir."

"The purpose of an investigation is not discovering facts."

"No?"

"No."

"Then what is it?"

"Creating time."

The Junior Officer wrote that down.

It seemed important.
