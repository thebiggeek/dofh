# DOFH #009
## The Dashboard

The Junior Officer rushed into the room.

"Sir, we have twelve active incidents."

"How many are visible?"

"Visible?"

"On the dashboard."

The Junior Officer checked.

"None."

"Excellent."

The Officer sipped his tea.

"Sir, one portal is down."

"Dashboard?"

"Green."

"Excellent."

"Two databases are unavailable."

"Dashboard?"

"Green."

"Three security incidents are under investigation."

"Dashboard?"

"Green."

The Officer smiled.

"Then I fail to see the problem."

The Junior Officer stared at the screen.

Every indicator was green.

Every system was broken.

"Sir, shouldn't the dashboard reflect reality?"

The Officer nearly spilled his tea.

"Absolutely not."

"Why not?"

"Reality changes."

"Yes."

"Dashboards provide stability."

The Junior Officer wrote this down.

It sounded insane.

Which usually meant it was policy.
