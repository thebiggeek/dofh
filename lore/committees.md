# Committee Terminology

## Committee

A mechanism for converting urgency into documentation.

---

## Subcommittee

A committee created to determine whether the original committee requires additional committees.

---

## Expert Committee

A committee whose members possess expertise in committee formation.

---

## High-Level Committee

A committee held in a larger room.

---

## Findings

Statements that everyone already knew.

---

## Recommendations

Suggestions unlikely to be implemented.

---

## Under Review

Nothing is happening.
