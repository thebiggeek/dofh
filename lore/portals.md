# Portal Terminology

## Portal

A website citizens are required to use.

---

## User Friendly

The portal has passed testing by the vendor.

---

## Scheduled Maintenance

Unexpected outage.

---

## Unscheduled Maintenance

Unexpected outage without prior notice.

---

## OTP

A six-digit number that expires before arrival.

---

## Dashboard

A mechanism for proving everything is operational.

---

## Green Status

The dashboard is functioning.

---

## System Load

An explanation.

Not a metric.
